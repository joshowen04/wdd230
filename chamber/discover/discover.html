Las Condes was Founded in 1901 on August 26th

The decision was made at the request of the owners of estates, who chose the emblem of Las Condes de Sierra Bella. According to some studies, there is the origin of the name of this territorial division.

1920 Towards the Urbanization of the Territory

In the 1920s, the urbanization of several sectors began. The land was leveled, wide avenues were opened, lines for drinking water, public and private electricity, and gas were laid. Added to this was the construction of elegant houses by prominent architects, such as Alberto Cruz Eyzaguirre.

Subsequently, the Hacienda Las Condes was subdivided. In 1949, the priests of the Dominican Recoleta sold the Apoquindo farm. This had been involved in litigation since 1936 and, after the sale, was divided into 2,094 sites, receiving the name of Villa Los Dominicos.

The city had arrived. Its inhabitants quickly promoted a new urban ideal, finding in the area the ideal setting for a new way of life.
