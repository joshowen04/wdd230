Week 2 - Design Principles

White Space and Clean Design

Quizlet

Quizlet's website

This site (or well a section of it) is designed specifically to give you the flash cards. I don't think you could put any more whitespace in a mobile webpage. What actually caught my attention was the absolute cleanliness of the design, It doesn't have any extraneous elements and takes you right where you want to go.

PARC: Repetition

Clinica Davila

Clinica Davila's website

The functionality of the page might be lacking, but I think this is a good representation of retpetition. All of the important elements have matching text formatting, colors, and spacing.

PARC: Contrast

IRS

IRS's website

While I am not a fan of this institution, I've had to spend quite a bit of time on the page recently and the contrast of the size and color of the print over the white page is easy to read. The menu takes it to the reverse with a blue background and white text, the font style is soft but clear and again, the readability is quite good in my opinion.
